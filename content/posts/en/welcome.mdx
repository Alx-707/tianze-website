# What Tianze Builds and Why Full-Chain Manufacturing Matters

When buyers look for conduit fittings or pneumatic tube products, they usually
see a crowded market of traders, factories, and mixed suppliers. Tianze takes a
different position.

We are not only selling finished products. We also build the **equipment**,
**molds**, and **forming process** behind those products. That matters because
it changes how quickly we can react, how consistently we can produce, and how
far we can go on custom requirements.

## Our Three Core Product Directions

### 1. PVC Conduit Fittings and Pipes

We manufacture conduit bends, sockets, couplings, straight pipes, and related
parts for different market standards and project needs.

### 2. Pipe Processing Equipment

Tianze develops and manufactures bending machines, expanders, cutters, and
tooling used in pipe processing. This is the engineering base behind our product
offer.

### 3. PETG Pneumatic Tube Systems

For medical and logistics applications, we also supply PETG pneumatic tubes,
bends, and connectors with an emphasis on transparency, stability, and fit-up
accuracy.

## Why This Model Helps Buyers

### Faster Response on Custom Work

Because the forming process is part of our own capability, custom bends and
non-standard parts are easier to assess and move into sampling.

### Better Process Control

Equipment, molds, and finished products are not being pushed across a chain of
unrelated suppliers. That reduces variation and speeds up troubleshooting.

### More Practical OEM Support

For buyers who need packaging adjustment, specification confirmation, or pilot
samples before scale, the direct factory model is easier to work with.
