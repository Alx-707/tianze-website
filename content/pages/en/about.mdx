## Who We Are

Tianze Pipe is a manufacturer focused on **PVC conduit fittings**, **PVC conduit
systems**, and **PETG pneumatic tube products**. Our advantage is practical: we
keep forming setup, mold work, and production control inside the factory.

That upstream control lets us respond faster on tooling, keep quality more
stable, and support custom projects that are hard for pure traders to handle.

## What We Make

### PVC Conduit Fittings and Pipes

We produce conduit bends, straight pipes, couplings, bell ends, double-socket
parts, and related fittings for multiple market standards.

### In-House Forming and Tooling

Our team prepares forming processes, custom molds, expanders, cutters, and
production fixtures used in our own conduit fittings workflow.

### PETG Pneumatic Tube Systems

We also supply high-transparency pneumatic tube products for hospital and
logistics applications, including straight tubes, bends, and connectors.

## Why Buyers Work With Tianze

### Process + Tooling + Fittings in One Chain

Because we control forming setup and tooling inside the factory, we can move
faster on difficult specifications, mold changes, and production setup.

### Flexible OEM and Custom Support

We support customer specifications, private label packaging, bend development,
and pilot sample work before larger production.

### Stable Export Supply

Tianze currently serves buyers in **20+ countries** with a focus on electrical
conduit and industrial pipe applications.

## How We Work

### 1. Understand the Requirement

We review the application, standard, bend form, diameter range, and packaging
requirement before proposing a solution.

### 2. Confirm Feasibility and Tooling

For standard products, we match existing production capability. For custom
parts, we review tooling, mold, and process requirements first.

### 3. Sample and Production

After sample confirmation, we move into stable production with batch control and
delivery planning.
