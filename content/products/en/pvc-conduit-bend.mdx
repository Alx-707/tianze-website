# PVC Electrical Conduit Bend

High-quality rigid PVC conduit bends manufactured to Schedule 80 specifications. Our bends feature precision molding and optional bell end (socket) processing for seamless installation.

## Key Features

- **Schedule 80 Wall Thickness**: Heavy-duty construction for demanding applications
- **Bell End Available**: Factory-expanded socket for easy joint connection
- **Smooth Interior**: Reduces friction during wire pulling
- **UV Stabilized**: Suitable for exposed outdoor installations
- **30+ Year Lifespan**: Corrosion-resistant PVC material

## Available Specifications

| Size | Angle Options | Wall Thickness | Bend Radius |
|------|---------------|----------------|-------------|
| 1/2" - 6" | 45°, 90° | Schedule 40/80 | Standard/Long |
| DN20-DN160mm | Custom angles | Per ASTM D1785 | Custom available |

## Standards Compliance

- **UL 651**: Listed for electrical conduit applications
- **ASTM D1785**: Standard specification for PVC pipe
- **AS/NZS 2053**: Australian/New Zealand standard
- **GB/T 5836**: Chinese national standard

## Applications

- Electrical wiring protection
- Underground cable runs
- Industrial facility wiring
- Commercial building construction
- Outdoor lighting installations
