## Application Scenarios

### Scenario A: Hospital Internal Logistics (医院内部物流传输)

**Applications**: Pharmacy dispensing, lab sample transport, ER document transfer

| Customer Pain Point | Tianze Solution |
|---------------------|-----------------|
| Sample damage/leakage during transport | **Leak-proof design** ensures biosafety |
| Opaque pipes make blockage diagnosis difficult | **PETG high-transparency** enables visual inspection |
| Noisy operation disturbs hospital environment | **Quiet operation** meets medical noise standards |

**Key Product**: PETG Pneumatic Tube System

---

### Scenario B: Commercial/Industrial Electrical Wiring (商业/工业电气布线)

**Applications**: Office building wire protection, factory workshop conduit pre-embedding

| Customer Pain Point | Tianze Solution |
|---------------------|-----------------|
| Pipes crushed/cracked during pre-embedding | **Schedule 80** heavy-duty pipes, high compression strength |
| Poor elbow seal leads to water ingress, wire corrosion | **Pre-fabricated bends** + **Expand mouth** design for tight fit |
| Low installation efficiency, frequent on-site hot bending | **Ready-made bends** (45°, 90°) speed up installation |
| Material degradation over time | **Corrosion-resistant PVC**, 30-year lifespan |

**Key Product**: PVC Electrical Conduit (Schedule 80)

---

### Scenario C: Municipal/Agricultural Water Supply (市政/农业给排水)

**Applications**: Agricultural irrigation, building drainage

| Customer Pain Point | Tianze Solution |
|---------------------|-----------------|
| Outdoor exposure causes pipe brittleness | UV-resistant, corrosion-resistant material |
| Chemical fertilizer/acid-base corrosion | **Corrosion-resistant** PVC formulation |
| Quality inconsistency from unknown suppliers | **ASTM standard** compliance, stable quality |

**Key Product**: PVC Water Supply Pipes
