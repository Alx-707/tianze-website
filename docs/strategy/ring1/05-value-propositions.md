## Company-Level Value Proposition

**For overseas buyers sourcing PVC conduit fittings, bending equipment, or custom molds, Tianze provides factory-direct products with full-chain manufacturing control — from the bending machines we build to the fittings we ship — unlike trading companies that resell without technical depth or factories that buy their equipment from others.**

### One-liner (for homepage hero):

**"We make the machines that make the pipes."**

### Supporting proof line:

"PVC conduit fittings, bending equipment, and custom manufacturing — all from one factory that controls the entire chain."

## Value Proposition Canvas Summary

### What buyers gain from choosing Tianze

| Gain | How Tianze delivers it |
|------|----------------------|
| Confidence in factory authenticity | "We make the machines" — visible manufacturing chain, not just a showroom |
| Competitive pricing | Factory-direct, no trading company markup |
| Quality verifiability | Free samples + test data + process transparency |
| Customization flexibility | In-house mold development + bending machine capability |
| Supply stability | Own production lines, not outsourced |
| Technical support | Engineering team that understands both machines and fittings |

### What pains Tianze relieves

| Pain | How Tianze addresses it |
|------|------------------------|
| "Is this a real factory or a trader?" | Equipment manufacturing background as proof |
| "Will quality be consistent?" | Process control from machine to finished product |
| "Can they handle non-standard specs?" | In-house mold development + flexible bending capability |
| "What about certifications?" | Transparent: "technology meets standards, certification in progress" + free samples to verify |
| "Communication and response time?" | Email-first response + technical team available |
| "What if there's a problem after delivery?" | Factory-direct accountability, not pass-through liability |

- Whether inquiry emails reference "machines" / "equipment manufacturer" language
- Whether the company narrative or product specs drive more inquiries
