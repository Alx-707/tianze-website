# Persona: OEM Buyer

> Business line: Custom Molds / OEM

## Profile

**Name archetype:** Sarah — Product Manager at a Building Materials Brand
**Company:** Regional building materials brand that outsources manufacturing
**Region:** North America, Europe, Australia
**Company size:** 20-100 employees, brand + distribution focused (no factory)
**Role:** Product Manager or Sourcing Director

## Job to Be Done

"Find a capable Chinese factory that can produce PVC fittings to our specifications under our brand — with the flexibility to handle non-standard sizes and the reliability to maintain quality across orders."

## Decision Chain

1. **Trigger:** New product line launch, or current OEM supplier can't meet new spec requirements
2. **Search:** Alibaba, trade shows, industry referrals, Google "PVC conduit OEM manufacturer China"
3. **Shortlist:** Based on factory capability proof, MOQ flexibility, previous OEM references
4. **Evaluate:** Request samples with custom specs, evaluate quality + communication + turnaround
5. **Decide:** Trial order (small batch), then long-term contract if quality proves consistent
6. **Others involved:** Quality assurance (testing), legal (supply agreement), marketing (packaging specs)

## Information Needs by Stage

| Stage | Needs | Content that answers |
|-------|-------|---------------------|
| Awareness | Does this factory do OEM? Can they develop custom molds? | OEM/Custom manufacturing page |
| Comparison | MOQ? Turnaround for new mold development? References? | Capability details, process explanation |
| Decision | IP protection? Quality control process? Contract terms? | Process transparency, QC documentation |

## Top 3 Concerns

1. **Mold development capability** — "Can you actually develop a mold from our drawings, or do you just run existing molds?"
2. **MOQ flexibility** — "We need 5,000 pieces first, not 50,000"
3. **IP protection** — "If I give you my specs, will they stay confidential?"

## How They Describe What They Want

- "We need a factory that can develop molds from our CAD files"
- "What's your minimum order for a custom product?"
- "How long from drawing to first sample?"
- "We need our logo molded into the product"
- "Do you have NDA / exclusivity agreements?"
