# Persona: PETG System Integrator

> Business line: Pipes (PETG Pneumatic Tubes)

## Profile

**Name archetype:** Dr. Chen — Hospital Logistics System Integrator
**Company:** Pneumatic tube system installation and maintenance firm
**Region:** Domestic China (primary), expanding to Southeast Asia
**Company size:** 30-100 employees, technical engineering team
**Role:** Technical Director or Supply Chain Manager

## Job to Be Done

"Find a PETG tube supplier with consistent transparency, zero-leak quality, and ability to handle custom specifications — so my pneumatic tube installations work flawlessly and I don't get called back for repairs."

## Decision Chain

1. **Trigger:** New hospital project contract won, or existing supplier quality drops
2. **Search:** Industry contacts, existing supplier network, trade shows
3. **Shortlist:** Based on tube transparency, seal quality test results, supply stability
4. **Evaluate:** Request samples, run pressure tests, check dimensional consistency
5. **Decide:** Based on test results + supply track record + price
6. **Others involved:** Hospital procurement (final approval), installation team (compatibility check)

## Information Needs by Stage

| Stage | Needs | Content that answers |
|-------|-------|---------------------|
| Awareness | Does this manufacturer specialize in PETG pneumatic tubes? | Dedicated PETG product section |
| Comparison | What's the transparency rating? Burst pressure? Custom diameter range? | Technical spec page with test data |
| Decision | Can they supply consistently for a multi-phase hospital project? | Production capacity info, reference projects (anonymized) |

## Top 3 Concerns

1. **Seal integrity** — "Zero leaks, non-negotiable — patient samples travel through these tubes"
2. **Transparency** — "We need to see blockages, and hospital clients expect visual quality"
3. **Custom specs** — "Every hospital layout is different, standard sizes rarely fit perfectly"

## How They Describe What They Want

- "We need OD 160mm PETG, wall thickness 3mm, cut to 6m lengths"
- "What's your burst pressure at 0.8MPa continuous?"
- "Can you supply 2,000 meters per month consistently?"
- "Our current supplier has quality fluctuations between batches"
