# Persona: Equipment Buyer (PVC Pipe Manufacturer)

> Business line: Equipment (Bending Machines)

## Profile

**Name archetype:** Ahmed — PVC Pipe Factory Owner
**Company:** PVC pipe extrusion factory, currently producing straight pipes only
**Region:** Middle East, Southeast Asia, Africa
**Company size:** 50-200 employees, established extrusion lines
**Role:** Factory Owner or Production Director

## Job to Be Done

"Add bending capability to my factory so I can sell pipe fittings (bends/elbows) in addition to straight pipes — my customers keep asking for bends and I'm losing them to competitors who offer full product ranges."

## Decision Chain

1. **Trigger:** Repeated customer requests for bends/elbows that Ahmed can't supply
2. **Search:** Google "PVC bending machine", Made-in-China, Alibaba, trade show contacts
3. **Shortlist:** Very few options (market scarcity validated by Task 2 research)
4. **Evaluate:** Technical specs, production capacity, ROI calculation, installation support
5. **Decide:** Based on ROI payback period + technical confidence + after-sales support
6. **Others involved:** Production manager (technical feasibility), finance (investment approval)

## Information Needs by Stage

| Stage | Needs | Content that answers |
|-------|-------|---------------------|
| Awareness | Who makes PVC bending machines? What's the investment range? | Equipment overview page |
| Comparison | What diameter range? Production rate? Power requirements? | Technical specs, machine evolution comparison |
| Decision | What's the ROI? Installation support? Training? Spare parts? | ROI calculator content, after-sales support info |

## Top 3 Concerns

1. **ROI justification** — "How many months to pay back the investment?"
2. **Technical reliability** — "Will this machine run 8 hours a day without problems?"
3. **After-sales support** — "If it breaks, who helps me fix it? Do you have engineers?"

## How They Describe What They Want

- "I already have extrusion lines, I need to add bending"
- "What's the maximum diameter your machine can handle?"
- "How many pieces per hour?"
- "Do you send engineers for installation and training?"
- "What spare parts should I stock?"
