# Persona: PVC Distributor

> Business line: Pipes (PVC Conduit)

## Profile

**Name archetype:** Mark — Regional Building Materials Distributor
**Company:** 3-8 retail locations selling electrical/plumbing supplies
**Region:** Australia, Southeast Asia, or Middle East
**Company size:** 10-50 employees, annual revenue $2-20M
**Role:** Owner or Procurement Manager

## Job to Be Done

"Find a reliable Chinese PVC conduit factory I can establish a long-term supply relationship with — good quality, competitive pricing, consistent delivery — so I can offer my retail customers better margins than buying from local wholesalers."

## Decision Chain

1. **Trigger:** Current supplier raises prices, quality drops, or Mark wants to expand product range
2. **Search:** Google "PVC conduit manufacturer China" + browse Alibaba + ask industry contacts
3. **Shortlist:** 3-5 suppliers based on certifications, product range, website professionalism
4. **Evaluate:** Request samples, compare specs and pricing, check factory credentials
5. **Decide:** Place trial order (1 container), evaluate delivery and quality, then scale
6. **Others involved:** Business partner (financial approval), warehouse manager (logistics)

## Information Needs by Stage

| Stage | Needs | Content that answers |
|-------|-------|---------------------|
| Awareness | Is this a real factory? What standards do they meet? | Homepage trust signals, about page |
| Comparison | How do specs compare? What's the pricing structure? MOQ? | Product detail pages, spec sheets |
| Decision | Can I trust the quality long-term? What if there's a problem? | Certifications, sample offer, factory tour invitation |

## Top 3 Concerns

1. **Quality consistency** — "Will batch #10 be as good as batch #1?"
2. **Certification compliance** — "Does this meet AS/NZS 2053 so I can sell it legally?"
3. **Communication reliability** — "Will they respond quickly when I have issues?"

## How They Describe What They Want

- "I need a factory, not a trader"
- "Show me the certificates"
- "What's your best price for a 20ft container?"
- "Can you do our private label?"
