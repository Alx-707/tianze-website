# Business Goals — Tianze Website

> Ring 1, Task 1 | Status: Confirmed by owner (2026-03-29)

## Primary Goal

**Generate high-quality inquiries from ideal end customers (distributors, contractors, manufacturers) via the website.**

"High-quality" defined as: inquiry from a target persona (per Ring 1 personas) with clear product need, not from trading companies reselling on margin.

## Conversion Goal Hierarchy

### Primary Conversions (directly generate business)

| Action | Where | Success metric |
|--------|-------|---------------|
| Submit inquiry form (quote request) | Contact page, product pages | # inquiries/month, inquiry-to-quote rate |
| Submit sample request | Product pages, homepage CTA | # sample requests/month |
| Book technical consultation | Equipment page, custom molds page | # bookings/month |
| WhatsApp contact initiation | Floating button, contact page | # conversations started/month |

**Preferred first-response channel: Email** (not WhatsApp). WhatsApp is secondary/supplementary.

### Secondary Conversions (indicate serious intent)

| Action | Where | Success metric |
|--------|-------|---------------|
| Download spec sheet / catalog PDF | Product detail pages | # downloads/month, download-to-inquiry rate |
| View certification status page | About page, product pages | Page views, time on page |
| Visit factory tour / capability page | About, equipment page | Page views |

### Micro-Conversions (engagement signals)

| Action | Where | Success metric |
|--------|-------|---------------|
| Product page depth > 2 pages | Product section | % of sessions with 2+ product pages |
| Time on product page > 60s | Product detail pages | Average time on page |
| Return visit within 7 days | Sitewide | % returning visitors |
| Blog article read-through | Blog | Scroll depth > 75% |

## Goal-to-Page Mapping

| Page | Primary goal | Secondary goal |
|------|-------------|---------------|
| Homepage | Route visitors to correct business line | Build initial trust |
| Products overview | Help buyer find relevant product category | Establish product range breadth |
| Product detail (PVC by market) | Drive inquiry or sample request | Provide downloadable specs |
| Product detail (PETG) | Drive inquiry for pneumatic tubes | Demonstrate technical capability |
| Equipment (bending machines) | Drive equipment inquiry or consultation | Validate "upstream manufacturer" story |
| OEM/Custom manufacturing | Drive custom project inquiry | Show mold development capability |
| About | Build trust and credibility | Support certification transparency |
| Contact | Convert visitor to inquiry | Capture lead information |
| Blog | SEO entry point, build authority | Drive to product/contact pages |
| FAQ | Remove purchase objections | Reduce inquiry friction |

## Success Metrics (Phase 1 targets)

| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|------------|
| Monthly inquiries (all channels) | Baseline: establish in first 3 months | GA4 form submission events + WhatsApp clicks |
| Inquiry quality rate | > 50% from target personas | Owner scores each inquiry: is this buyer someone we actually want to serve? (distributor/contractor/manufacturer = quality; trading company seeking lowest price = not quality) |
| Inquiry-to-quote conversion | Baseline: establish | Owner tracks manually |
| Avg. time to first inquiry response | < 24 hours | Owner tracks |
| Pages per session (product visitors) | > 2.5 | GA4 |
| Spec sheet download rate | > 5% of product page visitors | GA4 download events |

## What This Goal Is NOT

- Not brand awareness (that's a secondary effect, not the primary goal)
- Not e-commerce (no online ordering, all inquiry-based)
- Not content marketing for its own sake (blog exists to serve SEO which serves inquiry generation)
- Not competing with Alibaba (website targets Google/direct traffic; Alibaba handles platform traffic)
