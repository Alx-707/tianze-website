## Core Competitive Advantages

### 1. Systematic Bending Capability (核心)

Complete supply chain: **Equipment → Mold → Process → Fittings**

- Rapid mold development
- Quick production line setup
- Non-standard products at scale (bell-end bends, double-socket, large-arc cable pipes)
- Proven OEM capacity for tier-1 domestic brands

### 2. Equipment R&D Manufacturing

- Self-designed and manufactured bending machines, expanders
- Wide application in domestic and international PVC pipe processing
- Integrated expertise in equipment control, temperature control, mold systems

### 3. Non-standard Customization & Fast Prototyping

- Support for AU/US/CN standards
- Low MOQ for small customers and project samples
- Short drawing-to-sample cycle

### 4. Pneumatic Tube Service Capability

- High-transparency, high-seal pneumatic logistics tubes
- Materials: PVC, PMMA, PETG
- Custom bends and connectors
- Stable supply to major domestic system integrators

### 5. Integrated In-house Manufacturing

- Complete in-house: extrusion, bending, expansion, cutting
- Self-made key molds
- Rapid production line deployment
- Consistent quality and delivery reliability
