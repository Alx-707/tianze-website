Installation & Application

4 questions

Direct burial: Schedule 80 PVC conduit is rated for direct burial per NEC 300.5. No additional corrosion protection needed.

Concrete encasement: Both Schedule 40 and 80 can be encased. PVC's smooth interior allows easy wire pulling after curing.

FactorIndoorOutdoor
UV exposureNot a concernUV-stabilized PVC required
TemperatureStable (15-30 °C)Wide swings possible
Conduit typeSchedule 40 sufficientSchedule 80 recommended

Solar farms: Schedule 80 for underground cable runs, UV-resistant formulations for above-ground sections.

Data centers: LSZH options for raised-floor cable management, medium/heavy-duty IEC 61386 conduit for structured cabling pathways.

PVC has inherent chemical resistance: resistant to acids, alkalis, salt solutions, and common industrial chemicals. No galvanic corrosion in coastal/marine environments.

Unlike metal conduit, PVC requires no protective coatings, galvanizing, or cathodic protection — reducing installation cost and maintenance.
